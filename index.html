Crystal Collectors

You will receive a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your score.

If you successfully match your score to the random number given to you, you will win the game. If you exceed the number, you will lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click it, and it will change every round.

Let's get clicking! Press the start button below to begin.
